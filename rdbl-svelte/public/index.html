Re: Explicit Workflow

If you don’t have a documented workflow, you’d better have a fabulous internal vision for how you work, or insane luck in the behaviors department. Without a workflow, you might be able to harvest the cotton, but you’ll never be able to ship it across the globe, transforming at every port: seeds to fibers, fibers to threads, threads to cloth, cloth to died cloth, spools to T-shirts, T-shirts to printers, printers to wholesale, wholesale to retail, retail to resale, resale to landfill.

Of course, no one can manage that entire unclosed cycle (U vs O), but if your goal is to get from the raw cotton of your thoughts to the in-store jacket of a printed book you’re going to have to make it clear to yourself — crystal clear, and persistent, every day for the life of the project — exactly how you do what you do.

If only I knew how to follow my own advice!!
